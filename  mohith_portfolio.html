01 — About

Who I Am

I'm a Senior Data Engineer with over a decade of experience architecting data systems that don't just work — they scale, perform, and endure.

From designing multi-cloud data lakes to building real-time streaming pipelines with Kafka and Spark, I've delivered end-to-end data solutions for Fortune 500 companies across healthcare, fintech, retail, insurance, and e-commerce.

My edge: I bridge the gap between raw data chaos and clean, governed, analytics-ready infrastructure — all while keeping teams moving fast with CI/CD, Agile, and TDD.

I've led migrations from legacy on-prem systems to GCP BigQuery, Oracle to Azure Data Lake, and built ML pipelines for fraud detection on AWS SageMaker. Data is the product. I make sure it's excellent.
